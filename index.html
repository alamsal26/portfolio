About Me

Hello! I am Anish Lamsal. I am an Agricultural Engineer moving into the field of data analytics. My background connects hands-on engineering work with data problem solving to improve farm systems, manage building projects, and analyze complex information.

I worked for nearly three years as an Agricultural Engineer in Nepal. In that role, I designed food storage buildings, set up farm machinery, and handled project budgets. Working closely with rural supply chains made me realize how important real data is for making good business and policy decisions. Because of this, I completed an MBA to understand these problems from a business point of view.

Right now, I am studying for my Master of Science in Data Analytics at Binghamton University to grow my technical skills. My goal is to combine my engineering background with modern data tools to make supply chains run better and help organizations make smart decisions.
